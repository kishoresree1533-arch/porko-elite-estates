# Ultra-Realistic Luxury Villa Photography Specifications

- **Chennai ECR (East Coast Road)**
  - Premium beachfront properties
  - Modern architecture with ocean views
  - Infinity pools overlooking Bay of Bengal

- **Bangalore Outskirts (Whitefield, Sarjapur)**
  - Tech-elite gated communities
  - Contemporary villas with smart home integration
  - Lush landscaping with native flora

- **Hyderabad Jubilee Hills**
  - Ultra-luxury hilltop properties
  - Expansive city views
  - Premium gated communities

## Photography Requirements

### **Technical Specifications**
- **Camera**: Full-frame DSLR (Canon EOS R5, Sony A7R IV, Nikon Z9)
- **Resolution**: 8K (7680×4320) minimum
- **Format**: RAW files with professional editing
- **Lens**: 24-70mm f/2.8 for architectural shots
- **Lighting**: Golden hour (5:30-7:30 AM/PM)
- **Sky**: Deep blue with dramatic cloud formations

### **Architectural Elements**
- **Exterior**: Floor-to-ceiling glass walls, cantilevered balconies
- **Pool**: Infinity edge with reflection and underwater lighting
- **Landscaping**: Manicured lawns, palm trees, flowering plants
- **Materials**: Exposed teak wood, Italian marble, granite accents
- **Details**: Brass door hardware, custom metalwork, stone pathways

### **Interior Design Features**
- **Great Room**: 20-foot ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows
- **Kitchen**: Professional grade appliances, marble countertops
- **Master Suite**: Private balcony access, spa-like bathroom
- **Smart Home**: Integrated automation, concealed lighting, climate control
- **Materials**: Teak flooring, marble walls, brass fixtures

### **Composition Guidelines**
- **Leading Lines**: Use architectural elements to guide eye
- **Depth**: Include foreground, midground, background layers
- **Scale**: Human elements for perspective (people, cars, furniture)
- **Symmetry**: Break perfect symmetry for natural feel
- **Framing**: Rule of thirds for key architectural features

### **Lighting Requirements**
- **Natural**: Golden hour lighting with long shadows
- **Artificial**: Warm interior lighting (3000K temperature)
- **Reflections**: Pool water, glass surfaces, polished marble
- **Ambient**: Fill light from windows and skylights

### **Mood & Atmosphere**
- **Exclusive**: Gated community, private driveway, security presence
- **Calm**: Serene landscaping, water features, soft lighting
- **Powerful**: Elevated position, expansive views, premium materials
- **Legacy**: Traditional elements mixed with modern luxury

### **Post-Processing**
- **Color Grading**: Warm, rich tones with subtle blue hour shadows
- **Texture Enhancement**: Highlight wood grain, stone texture, fabric weaves
- **Imperfections**: Subtle lens flare, natural light falloff, realistic shadows
- **Sharpness**: Selective focus on architectural details, soft background

### **Environment Setup**
- **Location**: Premium gated communities with manicured grounds
- **Landscaping**: Native tropical plants, mature trees, flowering gardens
- **Weather**: Clear sky with dramatic clouds, no harsh midday sun
- **Access**: Ensure permission for photography in premium locations

### **Shot List**
1. **Exterior Establish**: Wide shot of entire villa at golden hour
2. **Architectural Detail**: Medium shots of unique design elements
3. **Pool & Landscape**: Reflection shots with tropical surroundings
4. **Interior Grandeur**: Great room with floor-to-ceiling features
5. **Luxury Details**: Close-ups of premium materials and craftsmanship
6. **Lifestyle Shot**: Pool area with sunset background
7. **Aerial View**: Drone shot showing property and community
8. **Night Scene**: Lit villa with warm interior lighting

### **Avoid**
- CGI renders or 3D visualizations
- Artificial or overly dramatic lighting
- Perfect, sterile compositions
- Over-saturation or unrealistic colors
- Generic stock photography look

### **Deliverables**
- **Primary Images**: 25+ edited 8K photographs
- **Video Content**: 4K architectural walkthrough footage
- **Detail Shots**: Material and craftsmanship close-ups
- **Aerial Media**: Drone footage and community views
- **Color Graded**: Professionally edited with consistent style
- **Web Ready**: Optimized for website and social media
