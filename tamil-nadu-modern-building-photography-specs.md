# Ultra-Realistic Tamil Nadu Modern Building Photography Specifications

## Location Targets
- **Chennai**: Modern IT corridor buildings, coastal contemporary architecture
- **Coimbatore**: Industrial modern buildings, textile district contemporary structures
- **Madurai**: Heritage-meets-modern architecture, temple-inspired contemporary designs
- **Trichy**: Modern educational institutions, contemporary residential complexes
- **Salem**: Modern industrial buildings, contemporary commercial architecture
- **Erode**: Modern textile industry buildings, contemporary residential developments

## Photography Requirements

### **Technical Specifications**
- **Camera**: Full-frame DSLR (Canon EOS R5, Sony A7R IV, Nikon Z9)
- **Resolution**: 8K (7680×4320) minimum
- **Format**: RAW files with professional editing
- **Lens**: 24-70mm f/2.8 for architectural shots, 16-35mm for wide exteriors
- **Lighting**: Natural sunlight, golden hour preferred (6:00-7:30 AM/PM)
- **Style**: Real estate photography, authentic documentary style

### **Architectural Elements to Capture**
- **Modern Indian Architecture**: Clean lines with traditional Tamil influences
- **Materials**: Local concrete, Chennai granite, Tamil Nadu tiles, teak wood
- **Environment**: Slightly dusty, authentic urban surroundings
- **Details**: Imperfect walls, realistic wear and tear, natural aging
- **Context**: Indian vehicles (autos, cars, bikes), native vegetation

### **Environmental Context**
- **Urban Surroundings**: Authentic Tamil Nadu street scenes
- **Native Trees**: Coconut trees (தென்னை), banana plants (வாழை மரம்), neem trees
- **Street Details**: Realistic Indian urban environment, local signage
- **Atmosphere**: Natural dust, authentic Indian city ambiance
- **Lighting**: Natural sunlight with realistic shadows and reflections

### **Composition Guidelines**
- **Perspective**: Eye-level architectural photography
- **Depth of Field**: Shallow depth for detail shots, deep for exteriors
- **Framing**: Include surrounding context for authenticity
- **Scale**: Human elements and vehicles for perspective
- ** asymmetry**: Break perfect symmetry for natural, realistic feel

### **Lighting Requirements**
- **Natural Sunlight**: Primary light source, authentic Indian sunlight quality
- **Golden Hour**: Early morning/late afternoon for warm, dramatic lighting
- **Shadows**: Realistic, soft shadows with natural falloff
- **Reflections**: Natural reflections on glass and polished surfaces
- **Ambient**: True-to-life ambient lighting conditions

### **Color & Mood**
- **Colors**: True-to-life colors, natural Indian sunlight warmth
- **Mood**: Natural, grounded, premium but realistic
- **Atmosphere**: Authentic Tamil Nadu urban environment
- **Texture**: Real material textures, no artificial smoothing

### **Specific Elements to Include**
- **Local Materials**: 
  - Chennai granite finishes
  - Local Tamil Nadu terracotta tiles
  - Teak wood with natural grain
  - Concrete with realistic texture
- **Urban Context**:
  - Indian vehicles (autos, Tata vehicles, motorcycles)
  - Local street furniture and signage
  - Native vegetation and landscaping
  - Realistic dust and weathering
- **Architectural Details**:
  - Modern interpretations of traditional Tamil elements
  - Local craftsmanship in building details
  - Climate-appropriate design features
  - Natural ventilation elements

### **Shot List**
1. **Exterior Wide**: Full building in urban context
2. **Architectural Detail**: Modern elements with Tamil influences
3. **Material Close-ups**: Local materials and craftsmanship
4. **Environmental Context**: Building in Tamil Nadu urban setting
5. **Street Level View**: Ground-level perspective with local context
6. **Golden Hour Shot**: Warm lighting on modern façade
7. **Detail Texture**: Close-ups of authentic materials
8. **Contextual Wide**: Building with surrounding Tamil Nadu environment

### **Post-Processing Guidelines**
- **Color Grading**: Natural, realistic Indian sunlight tones
- **Texture Enhancement**: Subtle enhancement of real material textures
- **Imperfections**: Maintain realistic wear, dust, and aging
- **Sharpness**: Selective focus on architectural details
- **Natural Look**: Avoid over-processing, maintain documentary feel

### **Quality Standards**
- **Resolution**: 8K minimum for all final images
- **Clarity**: Sharp, detailed architectural photography
- **Authenticity**: Real Indian urban environment, no staging
- **Consistency**: Consistent style across all images
- **Professional**: DSLR quality, professional editing

### **Strictly Avoid**
- **Foreign Architecture**: Western-style mansions, glass-only villas
- **Artificial Elements**: CGI, renders, overly processed images
- **Unrealistic Cleanliness**: Over-clean surfaces, perfect symmetry
- **Fantasy Lighting**: Unnatural or dramatic lighting effects
- **Non-Local Elements**: Foreign vehicles, inappropriate vegetation
- **AI Artifacts**: Any signs of AI generation or manipulation

### **Delivery Requirements**
- **Format**: High-resolution JPEG + RAW files
- **Quantity**: 20-30 images per building
- **Angles**: Multiple perspectives and compositions
- **Details**: Both wide shots and close-up details
- **Context**: Environmental and contextual shots included
- **Web Ready**: Optimized versions for website use

### **Tamil Nadu Specific Elements**
- **Cultural Context**: Authentic Tamil Nadu urban environment
- **Climate Adaptation**: Building designs suited to local climate
- **Local Craftsmanship**: Traditional skills in modern context
- **Urban Reality**: Real Indian city environment, not sanitized
- **Natural Aging**: Authentic wear and tear appropriate to age
