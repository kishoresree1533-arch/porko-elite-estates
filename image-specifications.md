# Ultra-Realistic South Indian Interior Design Image Specifications

## Technical Requirements
- **Resolution**: 8K (7680x4320) minimum, 4K acceptable
- **Format**: RAW files preferred, high-quality JPEG final delivery
- **Camera**: DSLR/Mirrorless with full-frame sensor
- **Lens**: Wide-angle (24-35mm) for interior shots
- **Lighting**: Natural ambient lighting with subtle artificial accents
- **Color Profile**: Natural, warm tones with accurate white balance

## Style Requirements

### Location Inspiration
- **Bangalore**: Contemporary luxury apartments, tech-optimized spaces
- **Chennai**: Traditional-meets-modern homes, coastal influences
- **Madurai**: Heritage-inspired luxury, temple architecture elements
- **Coimbatore**: Spacious villas, industrial-chic fusion

### Design Styles to Capture

#### 1. Contemporary Indian Minimalism
- Clean lines with subtle traditional elements
- Neutral color palette with earthy tones
- Teak wood furniture with minimalist designs
- Open floor plans with strategic furniture placement
- Indoor plants (peace lilies, snake plants, money plants)

#### 2. Modern Luxury with Traditional South Indian Elements
- Brass and gold accents in hardware and decor
- Traditional motifs in contemporary patterns
- Granite and marble finishes with local stone varieties
- Teak and rosewood furniture pieces
- Silk and cotton textiles with traditional weaving patterns

#### 3. Vastu-Compliant Layout
- Northeast-facing entrances and prayer spaces
- Central open spaces (brahmasthan)
- Strategic placement of water elements
- Natural light optimization
- Harmonious color schemes

## Specific Room Requirements

### Living Room
- Large windows with natural sunlight streaming in
- Teak wood coffee table and entertainment unit
- Plush sofas with premium fabric upholstery
- Subtle gold accents in light fixtures and decor
- Indoor plants in ceramic pots
- Marble or granite flooring with area rugs
- Soft shadows creating depth and dimension

### Kitchen
- Modular kitchen with teak wood cabinets
- Granite countertops with premium finishes
- Stainless steel appliances with integrated design
- Under-cabinet LED lighting
- Traditional brass utensils as decorative elements
- Spacious island with seating

### Bedroom
- Teak wood bed with carved headboard
- Premium mattress with luxury bedding
- Window treatments allowing natural light control
- Marble or granite bedside tables
- Subtle gold accents in lamps and hardware
- Indoor plants for air quality

### Bathroom
- Marble or granite walls and flooring
- Premium fixtures with gold/brass finishes
- Natural lighting through frosted windows
- Teak wood vanity with storage
- Premium toiletries in elegant dispensers

## Photography Guidelines

### Composition
- Eye-level perspective for natural feel
- Depth of field to focus on key elements
- Rule of thirds for balanced composition
- Leading lines to draw viewer's eye
- Include human elements for scale (optional)

### Lighting
- Golden hour lighting preferred (early morning/late afternoon)
- Soft, diffused natural light
- Avoid harsh shadows and overexposure
- Warm ambient lighting for evening shots
- Natural light patterns through windows

### Details to Capture
- Texture variations (wood grain, marble patterns)
- Realistic imperfections (slight wear, natural aging)
- Lived-in feel with subtle personal touches
- Reflections on polished surfaces
- Dust particles in light beams (subtle)

## Avoid
- CGI or overly processed look
- Cartoonish or artificial appearance
- Over-smooth surfaces
- Unrealistic lighting effects
- Fantasy or theatrical elements
- AI-generated artifacts

## Delivery Requirements
- 3-5 images per room category
- Multiple angles per space
- Detail shots of materials and craftsmanship
- Both wide shots and close-ups
- Color-accurate final images
- Web-optimized versions for website use

## Indian Context Specifics
- Local materials (Chennai granite, Bangalore marble)
- Traditional crafts (brass work, wood carving)
- Cultural elements (prayer spaces, traditional seating)
- Climate-appropriate design (ventilation, natural cooling)
- Local plant species and landscaping elements
