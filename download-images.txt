## How to Download:
1. Open your web browser
2. Go to each URL above
3. Right-click on the image and select "Save image as..."
4. Save to: c:\xampp\htdocs\porko-elite-estates\src\assets\
5. Use the exact filename specified

After downloading all images, the TrendingProjects component will automatically use them.
